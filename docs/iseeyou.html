The ICU Connection

The trail builder, an ICU (Intensive Care Unit) physician, chose a name rich with meaning. In English, "ICU" and "I See You" are pronounced identically — a pun made appropo given the deep care and individual attention central to intensive care medicine.

In Plains Cree the ICU would be expressed through words like sohkī-kanawēyimikamik (place of strong/intensive caring) or sohkī-nākatawihamākwān (place of intensive attention).

The act of truly seeing each patient—kiwâpamitin—embodies the essence of intensive care: acknowledging and attending to each person as a complete individual.
